Overview

As NPR’s in-house technology research & development center, NPR Labs provides a variety of tools to assist public broadcasters nationwide. Its Mapping And Population System (MAPS) is an interactive application that provides terrain-sensitive coverage maps for reception of all public radio and television stations in the U.S.

During my internship in the spring of 2017, I worked as part of a small team to build a new version of the tool from the ground up.
