Powell's & The Nature Conservancy

Heuristic Reviews, Research, & Prototyping

Maryland Institute College of Art, Fall 2017

User Research Axure

Overview

Some recent coursework from my graduate studies at Maryland Institute College of Art (MICA) from the course, Foundations of User Experience Design. This course was an overview of the UX field, as well as an introduction to rapid prototyping in Axure, user interviews, and usability testing.

Below are pieces from two projects. The first is user journey map based on a heuristic review of Powells.com, a bookselling website. Following that is a medium-fidelity redesign proposal for Nature.org, based on in-person card sorting of navigation categories. Included are the results of the card sorting and subsequent usability testing of the prototype.
