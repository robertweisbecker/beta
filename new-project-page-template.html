Problem: Hide and Seek

Navigating BBV's current website feels like a game of hide and seek.

Whether the user is an experienced player or newcomer, it is difficult to find relevant information in a timely way.

This is a result of visual clutter and unclear content organization.


Solution: Find and Accomplish

Our goal was to refine the site's design and structure to allow users to find relevant information through top-down processing-- moving from general to specific.

Through our research, we aimed to imrpove the experience of using the BBv website and increase its utility within day-to-day business operations.


Research Phase

Site Analytics

87% of users navigate directly to the homepage.

Clarity of homepage is crucial to users finding what they need.

52.6% of users spend less than 30 seconds on the site.

Information must be accessed quickly, be readily apparent.

Over 60% of users access the site via mobile.

Site must be responsive/mobile friendly.
