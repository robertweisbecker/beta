Utility

Acme Fire

Acme Fire sells fire detection systems, with a selection of over 500 models. The models have different voltage, color, decibel level and lifespan. They offer systems that work for single level dwellings to twenty floor solutions. Some of their systems come with one - eighty sensor systems. The sensors include smoke detection, heat sensors, UV and IR flame detectors. Some systems also come with sprinkler and flame retardant foam capabilities.

These systems can only be sold to certified professionals. In order to become a certified professional a user must enter their fire inspector or installer state id to continue with an online order.

They offer sixteen different manufacturers, two of the manufacturer do not sell products to residents of California. One manufacturer only sells in the Tri State region of PA, NY and NJ. Acme’s product pages offer user reviews with a five star system. They also offer “certified” professionals a different type of rating system based on A+-D-. Both certified professionals and regular reviewers need to sign in to rate a product. Only certified professionals can see other certified professionals ratings.
